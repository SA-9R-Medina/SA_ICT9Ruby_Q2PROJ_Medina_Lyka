Delicious Strawberry Cake

Enjoy this light and fluffy strawberry cake with a burst of fresh flavor!

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
All-purpose flour 2 1/2 cups
Sugar 1 1/2 cups
Baking powder 2 1/2 tsp
Salt 1 tsp
Eggs 3 large
Milk 1 cup
Vegetable oil 1/2 cup
Vanilla extract 1 tsp
Fresh strawberries, pureed 1 cup
Boiling water 1/2 cup

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease and flour two 9-inch round baking pans.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, beat the eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla extract together until smooth.
  4. Mix the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until well combined.
  5. Stir in the pureed strawberries, then gradually add the boiling water to the batter. The batter will be thin.
  6. Divide the batter evenly between the prepared pans and smooth the tops.
  7. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  8. Allow the cakes to cool in the pans for 10 minutes, then remove them and cool completely on a wire rack.
  9. Frost with your favorite strawberry frosting or whipped cream, decorate as desired, and enjoy!
